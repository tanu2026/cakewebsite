Our Story

Born from a Love of Baking

Crumble & Co began in a small home kitchen in Hyderabad in 2015. Our founder, Meera Reddy, started with one simple belief: every occasion deserves a cake made with genuine care and the finest ingredients.

What started as weekend orders for neighbours and friends quickly grew into a beloved local institution. Today, we bake over 200 cakes a week, but each one still receives the same personal attention as that very first batch.

We source our dairy locally, use only natural flavours, and never rush the process. Baking is our art — and you are our canvas.
